Inventory

Actions

  • d or description - Description of the current room.
  • things - List of things in current room.
  • go <direction> - Moves you in the direction specified (north, south, east, or west).
  • look <thing> or l <thing> - Description of thing.
  • take <thing> or t <thing> - Takes thing and puts it in your inventory chest.
  • use <thing> or u <thing> - Uses the specified thing (but only if it is in your inventory chest!).
  • give <thing> or g <thing> - Gives the specified item to Larry.
  • remove <thing> or r <thing> - Removes the specified thing from your inventory.
  • play <number> or p <number> - Plays the specified game in the casino.
